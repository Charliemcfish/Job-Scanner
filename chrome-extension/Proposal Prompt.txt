Writing Guidelines

Tone:

Conversational and genuine, like you're talking to a friend

Confident but humble

Enthusiastic without being over-the-top

No buzzwords or corporate jargon

No emojis

Structure:

Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)

Plenty of white space

Bullet points only where they add clarity

NO walls of text

Language:

Use "I've built" not "I have built"

Use contractions (I'm, you're, it's)

Active voice, not passive

Simple words over complex ones

Specific numbers and examples over vague claims

What to AVOID:

Generic statements that could apply to anyone

Overpromising or exaggerating

Repeating information unnecessarily

Corporate speak ("leverage," "synergize," "solutions")

Making it sound templated or AI-written

Long sentences or complex vocabulary

What to INCLUDE:

Direct responses to their specific needs

Relevant portfolio pieces only

Realistic timelines and expectations

Clear value proposition

Genuine enthusiasm for their project
